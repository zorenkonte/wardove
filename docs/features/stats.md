# Stats

The Stats screen gives you analytics about your wardrobe, all computed locally from your wear logs.

## Summary metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Total items | Count of all clothing items in your wardrobe |
| Total wears | Sum of all wear log entries |
| Most worn | Item with the highest total wear count |
| Least worn | Item with the lowest total wear count |
| Longest unworn | Oldest clean item (the one that has sat in your wardrobe the longest without being worn) |
| Avg wears per item | Total wears ÷ total items |

## Cost per wear

Lists every item that has **a price set** and **at least one wear**, sorted by cost-per-wear (price ÷ wear count). A lower cost-per-wear means you're getting more value from a piece.

Items without a price or with zero wears are excluded.

## Category breakdown

Shows how many items you have in each category: Top, Bottom, Shoes, Outerwear, Accessory.
