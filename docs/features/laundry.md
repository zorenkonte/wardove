# Laundry

The Laundry screen manages your dirty pile and wash cycles. It has two tabs.

## Pile tab

Shows all items with status **Worn** — your current dirty pile.

**Wear threshold** — each item displays a flag when its total wear count has reached or exceeded the threshold. The threshold is adjustable with **+** / **−** buttons (range 1–30, default is configurable). Use it as a "time to wash" hint.

**Sending to laundry:**
1. Tap items to select them (multi-select).
2. Tap **Send to laundry**.

Selected items move to an active wash cycle and their status changes to **In Laundry**.

## Washing tab

Shows all **active** (in-progress) laundry cycles. Each cycle lists the items inside it.

Tap **Complete cycle** to finish a wash:
- All items in the cycle become **Clean**.
- Wear counts reset to zero.
- The cycle is stamped with a completion time and moves to [History](./laundry#history).

## History

Tap the history link (top-right of the Laundry screen) to see all **completed** cycles. Tap any cycle to expand it and see which items were in that wash.
